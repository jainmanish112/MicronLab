Dr. Manish Jain

Principal Investigator

Email: manish.jain@nmt.edu

Dr. Manish Jain is an Assistant Professor (2025 - Present) in the Department of Materials and Metallurgical Engineering at New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology, with a joint faculty appointment at Sandia National Laboratories. He earned his Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering from the University of Nevada, Reno (2019), and previously held postdoctoral positions at Sandia National Laboratories (2023 - 2025), the University of New South Wales (2021 - 2022), and Empa, the Swiss Federal Laboratories for Materials Science and Technology (2019 - 2021). His research focuses on hierarchical materials design, structure-property correlations under extreme conditions, thin film synthesis, and machine learning-assisted high-throughput materials discovery.
